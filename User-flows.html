Way to show cart in watch

Apple watch has the unique interaction, which allow me to hide the cart on the screen first. If user want to see the cart, they will need to long press the screen.

Browse but can't buy products

Due to the fact that you can not type in the apple watch, you can browse and design the bike. However, when you need to fill in and pay for the bike, you will need to move into desktop or mobile to finish the payment.
